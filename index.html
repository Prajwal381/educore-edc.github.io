About EduCore

EduCore (EDC) is a Polygon-based utility token designed to support education-focused initiatives and digital learning ecosystems. The project explores the use of blockchain technology to provide transparent access to learning resources and educational tools.

Token Utility

EDC functions as a digital access token within the EduCore ecosystem. Users may obtain EDC directly from the project or through participation-based mechanisms and use it to unlock educational content, tools, and learning services.

Future updates may expand EduCore into certification systems, reward mechanisms, and education-focused infrastructure platforms.
